About Prism

Prism Physiotherapy Centre is one of the best centres for pain relief and physiotherapy . We specialize in effective treatment of cervical spondylitis, frozen shoulder, tennis elbow, lumbar spondylosis and facial palsy. Our special expertise includes stroke rehabilitation, osteo arthritis, neuro muscular disorders, gait training and post operative stiffness. All type of joint pain and muscle pain management are provided with complete customer satisfaction.
